--- SKILLED MIGRATION VISA — ADELAIDE ---

H3: Skilled Migration Visa — Adelaide

South Australia has one of Australia's most proactive and accessible state
nomination programs for skilled migrants. The South Australian Government
actively encourages skilled professionals to choose Adelaide as their home —
and the state's growing defence, technology, healthcare, construction, agriculture,
and wine industry sectors mean there is strong and sustained demand for skilled
workers across a wide range of occupations.

Our best migration agents in Adelaide for skilled visas assist with:

- Subclass 189 — Skilled Independent Visa:
  A points-tested permanent visa for skilled workers who do not need a state
  sponsor or employer. Ideal for high-scoring professionals on Australia's
  Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL) or the Short-term
  Skilled Occupation List (STSOL) with sufficient points.

- Subclass 190 — Skilled Nominated Visa (South Australia Nomination):
  South Australia nominates skilled workers whose occupations align with the
  state's economic priorities and workforce needs. A successful SA nomination
  adds 5 points to your SkillSelect EOI score — a valuable advantage in a
  competitive invitation round.

- Subclass 491 — Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa:
  An excellent pathway for skilled workers willing to live and work in regional
  South Australia. SA's regional areas — including the Barossa Valley, Riverland,
  Limestone Coast, Eyre Peninsula, Yorke Peninsula, and Flinders Ranges — offer
  strong employment opportunities and a clear pathway to the Subclass 191
  permanent regional visa after three years.

Our Adelaide migration agents will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your
points score, occupation on the relevant skilled occupation list, skills assessment
body requirements, and English language results — building the strongest possible
Expression of Interest for your SkillSelect profile.
