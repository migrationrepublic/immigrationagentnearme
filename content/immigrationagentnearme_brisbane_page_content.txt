--- SKILLED MIGRATION VISA — BRISBANE ---

H3: Skilled Migration Visa — Brisbane

Queensland's growing economy and the infrastructure surge ahead of the 2032
Brisbane Olympics have created strong demand for skilled professionals across
engineering, construction, healthcare, information technology, education,
hospitality, and many other industries. Brisbane is an outstanding destination
for skilled migrants — and our best migration agents in Brisbane are experts
at navigating the skilled visa system.

We assist Brisbane applicants with:

- Subclass 189 — Skilled Independent Visa:
  A points-tested permanent visa for skilled workers with no requirement for
  a state or employer sponsor. Ideal for high-scoring professionals on
  Australia's Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL).

- Subclass 190 — Skilled Nominated Visa (Queensland Nomination):
  Queensland nominates skilled workers in occupations that align with the
  state's economic priorities. A successful Queensland nomination adds 5
  points to your SkillSelect EOI score, increasing your chances of receiving
  an invitation to apply.

- Subclass 491 — Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa:
  A provisional visa for skilled workers willing to live and work in regional
  Queensland. After three years on the 491, eligible holders can apply for the
  Subclass 191 permanent regional visa.

Our Brisbane migration agents will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your
points score, occupation on the skilled occupation list, skills assessment
requirements, and English language results to build the most competitive EOI
possible in SkillSelect.
