H3: Skilled Migration Visa — Canberra

Canberra and the ACT offer outstanding opportunities for skilled migrants. The
territory's economy is driven by the federal public sector, defence and
intelligence agencies, information technology, healthcare, education, and a
rapidly expanding private sector — all of which maintain strong and consistent
demand for skilled professionals. The ACT Government's skilled migration
nomination program reflects this demand, regularly inviting skilled workers
across a wide range of occupations.

Our best migration agents in Canberra for skilled visas assist with:

- Subclass 189 — Skilled Independent Visa:
  A points-tested permanent visa for skilled workers who do not need a state
  or territory sponsor or an employer. Ideal for high-scoring professionals
  listed on Australia's Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL).

- Subclass 190 — Skilled Nominated Visa (ACT Nomination):
  The ACT Government nominates skilled workers whose occupations align with
  the territory's economic and workforce priorities. A successful ACT nomination
  adds 5 points to your SkillSelect EOI score — a crucial advantage in securing
  an invitation to apply for permanent residency.

- Subclass 491 — Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa:
  Canberra and the ACT — along with surrounding regional New South Wales —
  qualify as regional areas under Australia's regional migration framework.
  This makes the 491 an excellent pathway for skilled workers looking to settle
  in the Canberra region, with a clear route to the Subclass 191 permanent
  regional visa after three years.

Our Canberra migration agents will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your
points score, occupation eligibility, skills assessment body requirements, and
English language results — and will build the most competitive Expression of
Interest possible for your SkillSelect profile.
