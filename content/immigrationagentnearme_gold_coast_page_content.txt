H3: Skilled Migration Visa — Gold Coast

The Gold Coast's rapidly expanding economy — driven by construction, tourism
and hospitality, healthcare, technology, education, and the upcoming 2032
Brisbane Olympic events — creates strong and sustained demand for skilled
professionals from overseas. Our best migration agents on the Gold Coast for
skilled visas assist with:

- Subclass 189 — Skilled Independent Visa:
  A points-tested permanent visa for skilled workers with no requirement for
  a state or employer sponsor. Ideal for high-scoring professionals on
  Australia's Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL).

- Subclass 190 — Skilled Nominated Visa (Queensland Nomination):
  Queensland nominates skilled workers whose occupations align with the
  state's workforce and economic priorities. A successful Queensland nomination
  adds 5 points to your SkillSelect EOI score — a significant advantage in
  securing an invitation to apply.

- Subclass 491 — Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa:
  The Gold Coast and surrounding areas of South East Queensland may qualify
  as regional areas for the purposes of the 491 visa. This provisional visa
  provides a pathway to the Subclass 191 permanent regional visa after three
  years of living and working in a qualifying regional area.

Our Gold Coast migration agents will conduct a thorough assessment of your
points score, occupation eligibility, skills assessment requirements, and
English language results — building the strongest possible Expression of
Interest for your SkillSelect profile.
