H3: Skilled Migration Visa — Melbourne

Melbourne and Victoria offer outstanding opportunities for skilled migrants.
Victoria's economy spans healthcare, education, construction, information technology,
engineering, finance, and professional services — all industries with strong demand
for skilled overseas workers.

Our best migration agents in Melbourne for skilled visas assist with:

- Subclass 189 — Skilled Independent Visa:
  A points-tested permanent visa for skilled workers who do not need a state or
  employer sponsor. Ideal for high-scoring professionals on Australia's Medium and
  Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL).

- Subclass 190 — Skilled Nominated Visa (Victoria Nomination):
  Victoria nominates skilled workers in occupations that meet the state's economic
  needs. Receiving Victorian nomination adds 5 points to your SkillSelect score and
  significantly strengthens your invitation to apply.

- Subclass 491 — Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa:
  A pathway for skilled workers willing to live and work in regional Victoria.
  After three years, 491 holders can apply for the Subclass 191 permanent visa.

Our Melbourne migration agents will conduct a thorough assessment of your points
score, occupation on the relevant skilled occupation list, skills assessment body
requirements, and English language proficiency to build the strongest possible
Expression of Interest in SkillSelect.
