--- SKILLED MIGRATION VISA — PERTH ---

H3: Skilled Migration Visa — Perth

Western Australia has one of Australia's most active and well-resourced skilled
migration programs. WA's mining, resources, oil and gas, construction, engineering,
healthcare, and agriculture sectors create a consistently high demand for skilled
overseas workers — and the Western Australian Government's state nomination program
reflects this demand with regular and competitive nomination rounds.

Our best migration agents in Perth for skilled visas assist with:

- Subclass 189 — Skilled Independent Visa:
  A points-tested permanent visa requiring no state sponsor or employer.
  Ideal for skilled professionals scoring highly on Australia's points test
  and listed on the Medium and Long-term Strategic Skills List (MLTSSL).

- Subclass 190 — Skilled Nominated Visa (Western Australia Nomination):
  The WA Government nominates skilled workers in occupations that align
  with the state's economic and workforce priorities. A successful WA
  nomination adds 5 points to your SkillSelect EOI score — a significant
  advantage in a competitive invitation round.

- Subclass 491 — Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa:
  An excellent pathway for skilled workers willing to live and work in
  regional Western Australia. WA's vast regional areas — including the
  Pilbara, Kimberley, Goldfields, Wheatbelt, South West, and Great
  Southern regions — offer strong employment opportunities and a clear
  pathway to permanent residency through the Subclass 191 visa after
  three years.

Our Perth migration agents will conduct a thorough assessment of your points
score, occupation eligibility, skills assessment body requirements, and English
language test results — building the most competitive Expression of Interest
possible for your SkillSelect profile.
